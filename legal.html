Legal

The Jacksonville Linux Users Group Inc. legal information, state and federal corporate and trademark filings.

Corporate

The Jacksonville Linux Users Group Inc. is a legal Florida Non-Profit corporation, registered as 501(c)(3) under the IRC with the IRS.

State

The Jacksonville Linux Users Group Inc. is a Florida Non-Profit Corporation document number N25000011929. A fictitious name "JaxLUG" has also been filed under registration number G25000122462. Form DR-5 has been filed with the Florida Department of Revenue.

Federal

The Jacksonville Linux Users Group Inc. is a United States Non-Profit Corporation federal employer identification number (EIN) 39-4420367. We have received Letter 947 from the IRS confirming federal income tax exception under the IRC Section 501(c)(3); Public charity status 170(b)(1)(A)(vi). Donors can deduct contributions they make under IRC Section 170, along with tax deductible bequests, devises, transfers, or gifts under Sections 2055, 2106, or 2522.
